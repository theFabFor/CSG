Multi-modal Transportation

Railyard - Colorado Sweetgold Industrial Park has over 2 miles of track servicing our trans-loading facility, industrial buildings, bottom dump rail car platform, oil storage tanks & grain elevators. The yard has two manned locomotives providing the ability to position cars for pickup by Great Western, a short line railroad, connecting our facilities to Union Pacific & BNSF both a few short miles away.

Highway access - Our main entrance is off of State Highway 50 connecting the industrial park to Interstate I25 which lies 6 miles to the east & Interstate I85, a short 10 miles west of Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park.

Denver International Airport - Denver International is a quick 48 minute drive from Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park. Twenty-five passenger airlines servicing the nations 5th largest airport provide easy access to almost any destination worldwide.

Water Sales - Through Colorado Big Thompson Water District & Hillsborough Consolidated Ditch Company, Colorado Sweet Gold has the ability to meet the most demanding industrial and agricultural water needs.

Water Storage - Two water storage ponds totalling (4 million gallons) ensures availability 24/7.

Water Loading - Colorado Sweet Gold facilities feature a (300 gallon a minute) automated water loading platform with room for multiple pumping stations if needed. Easy access for single or double tractor trailer rigs.

Raw Ground - One acre to 100 acre plots of open ground are available for ag and/or industrial uses. Equipment storage, staging areas as well as irrigated annual crop land. See Build to suit section for longer term solutions.

Existing Structures - Five thousand to 33,000 square feet of warehouse space on site. Machanic shop with 3 phase 440v electricity, natural gas heat, ventilation system as well as office with restrooms available for lease.

Build to suit - If one of our existing facilities doesn't completely fulfill your needs call us at (970) 587-6528 to discuss long term build to suit options. Alternatively send us an email at land@coloradosweetgold.com.

Electric Power - In 2009 Northern Electric company partnered with Colorado Sweet Gold to build a new sub-station that services both Johnstown and CSG. With a total output of 4gW of power Our neighborhood will enjoy ample, reliable power well into the 21st century.

City Utilities - The proximity of Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park to the city of Johnstown gives our industrial park a unique ability to offer city services including sewer service and city water supply reducing the up front costs of establishing a presence by eliminating the need for wells and septic fields.

Natural Gas - CSG enjoys the benefits of a natural gas supply capable of delivering 3.2 million BTUs per hour through a 4" 80psi supply line.

Parish Annex

The Western most 62 acres of the Colorado Sweet Gold property has been annexed by the city of Johnstown. Provisions for both commercial and residential uses is approved. The master plan is currently under development. For more information regarding this project email parishAnnex@coloradosweetgold.com or call us at (970)587-6528

City Center - Directly across Parish Road from the annex, the city of Johnstown has recently completed the construction of their new town hall, police facility and Library. For more information click here.

Phased Development - Following the master plan, development will proceed following a phased approach. Initial construction will include residential lots designed to provide easy access to Johnstown's city center as well as the planned commercial portion of Parish Annex. For more information click here.

Nature Trail - Parish Annex is located near the planned nature trail connecting Johnstown to nearby Milliken.
