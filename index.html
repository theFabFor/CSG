Colorado Sweet Gold Facilities

Railyard - Colorado Sweetgold Industrial Park has over 2 miles of track inter-connecting our trans-loading facilities, industrial buildings, bottom dump rail car platform, oil storage tanks & grain elevators with Colorado's Great Western short line railroad. The yard is serviced by two manned locomotives providing the ability to position cars for pickup by Great Western who in turn connect our facilities to the Union Pacific & BNSF railroads.

Highway access - Our main entrance is off of State Highway 50 connecting the industrial park to Interstate I25 which lies 6 miles to the east & Interstate I85, a short 10 miles west of Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park.

Denver International Airport - Denver International is a quick 48 minute drive from Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park. Twenty-five passenger airlines servicing the nations 5th largest airport provide easy access to almost any destination worldwide.

Electric Power - In the mid 1990s the Northern Electric company partnered with Colorado Sweet Gold to build a new sub-station that services both Johnstown and CSG. With a total output of 64mW of power our facility enjoys ample, reliable power.

Water Sales - Through Colorado Big Thompson Water District & Hillsborough Consolidated Ditch Company, Colorado Sweet Gold has the ability to meet the most demanding industrial and agricultural water needs.

Water Storage - Storage ponds totalling 183 acre feet ensures availability 24/7.

Water Loading - Colorado Sweet Gold facilities feature a 600 gallon a minute automated water loading platform with room for multiple pumping stations if needed. Easy access for single or double tractor trailer rigs.

City Utilities - The proximity of Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park to the city of Johnstown gives our industrial park a unique ability to offer city services including sewer service and city water supply reducing the up front costs of establishing a presence.

Bottom dump railcar facility - CSG has a bottom dump off loading station capable of transferring dry bulk goods from rail car to tractor trailer rigs.

Grain elevator - A 75,000 bushel capacity grain elevator and ancillary equipment are in operation at Colorado Sweet Gool Industrial Park. This facility is under an exclusive lease till summer of 2019.

Liquid Storage Facility - Two million gallon storage tanks are available as well as a smaller 350,000 gallon solution. All have rail and truck access. Visit the contact page for more information.

Raw Ground - Over 100 acres of open ground are available for equipment and material storage, staging areas and irrigated crop land. CSG also offers longer term build to suit solutions.

Existing Structures - From a 3,020 square foot maintenance shop to 18,439 square feet of warehouse space CSG has a variety of options available for lease. Many of these buildings are equiped with 3 phase 440v electricity and natural gas heat as well as offices and restrooms.

Build to suit - If one of our existing facilities doesn't completely fulfill your needs call us at (970) 587-6528 to discuss long term build to suit options. Alternatively send us an email at land@coloradosweetgold.com.

Natural Gas - CSG enjoys the benefits of a natural gas supply capable of delivering 11 million BTUs per hour.

Colorado Sweet Gold Operations

Support Services - Colorado Sweet Gold has an extended line of industrial support services. We have the infrastructure in place to get the job done.

Maintenance - Our in house maintenance team covers a wide range of common industrial needs. Welding, fabrication, backhoe services, tractor work and more. On site staff housing ensures high availability as well as a more secure facility.

Transloading - Two separate transloading stations are available. Over two miles of railyard allow for the option of short term tank car storage. A 2.3 million gallon storage facility is also available during times of limited rail car availability.

Manned Locomotives - CSG has two on site locomotives ready to perform rail car positioning, unit makeup, loading & un-loading.

Scalehouse - Our scalehouse is open 7 days a week. Monday through Friday 6am to 6pm, Saturday 11am through 5pm, and Sunday 6am through 5pm.

Work Force - From locomotive engineers to equipment operators, certified weighmasters, welders and office management personnel, Colorado Sweet Gold is proud to have an exceptional organization in place to support our clientele.

Parish Annex

The Western most 62 acres of the Colorado Sweet Gold property has been annexed by the city of Johnstown. Provisions for both commercial and residential uses is approved. Phased Development - Following the master plan, development will proceed in a phased approach. Initial construction includes residential lots designed to provide easy access to Johnstown's new city center as well as the planned commercial portion of Parish Annex. For more information click here.

City Center - Directly across Parish Road from the annex the city of Johnstown has recently completed the construction of their new town hall, police facility and library.

Nature Trail - Parish Annex is located near the proposed nature trail connecting Johnstown to nearby Milliken.

About Colorado Sweet Gold Industrial Park

Colorado Sweet Gold's 21st century mission is to invest in appropriate uses of our facilities to provide core resources for our regional partners. We are fortunate to be located in the dynamic North Colorado economic region where vast new oil and natural gas reserves are driving the local economy to previously unheard of levels.

CSG Industrial Park resides at the former Great Western Sugar company's Johnstown facility and has been able to repurpose some of their original infrastructure, particularly the railyard. Much of the original infrastructure, outdated and worn, has been recycled. What has been preserved is the foundation for our vision of this historic property in today's Johnstown Colorado.

We understand the importance of sustainability and how devastating it can be to a region when outside forces cause the collapse of entire business sectors, much as what became of the midwest's sugar beet industry. With that in mind we have developed a plan for this property that is diverse, takes into account the needs of the local community, and puts back to work a significant portion of the property.

Approximately 20% of the land has been annexed into Johnston and, in coordination with local planners, is being developed into new residential and commercial neighborhoods. With a focus on easy access to the new city center and proximity to the proposed nature trail connecting Johnstown to nearby Milliken we hope this development appeals to Coloradans who enjoy the conveniences that a town the size of Johnstown provides while still being in touch with the region's natural beauty.

The remaining 80% of the property is presently used for a variety of purposes, from supporting our vibrant oil industry via a transloading facility and oil storage tank farm to grain elevators, heated warehousing, water storage facilities, small business operating centers, a certified scalehouse, and more. In addition over 50 acres are in production as irrigated farm land. The north east corner houses Johnstown's electrical sub-station, providing reliable cost effective energy to the town.

We would like to believe we're earning a place in the community by bringing back to life a property that might just as easily become the permanent victim of disuse and decay. Along the way in the midst of the hard work of building sustainable businesses, new aquaintenances will be made, challenges will be met, loss will be mourned, and success will be celebrated. We're prepared for all of those outcomes. If our business sense and offerings are of interest give us a call. North Colorado is ready.
